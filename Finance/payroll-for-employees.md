# Payroll: Employee How To

Here's what you need to know and what you can expect from Payroll at Hypha.

## As a new employee

We need the following from you:

* When you've received an email from Wagepoint, log in and set up:
  * your password
  * your "My Info" profile
  * your "Bank Info" details by clicking on "Add Bank Account"

It's that simple!

> The Operations Working Group will fill out your "Work Info" for you.

## Paydates

At Hypha, we are paid monthly on the 15th of the month, or the previous working day before that date if that date falls on a weekend or holiday.

## Collecting your paystub

On each payday, you will receive an email from Wagepoint to your personal email address, which will include a link to log in.

Once you've logged in, you will be in your Personal Dashboard.

* You'll see "Payroll History" on the right-hand side of your screen, followed by a list of all current and prior paydates.
* You can click on "Details" next to any of the pay dates listed to see your paystub.
* You can print or download each paystub as you wish.

> If you do not wish to receive an email notification each payday, you can opt out by going to the "My Info" tab, scrolling down, and editing the "Email Notifications" option.

## When things change

### Your home address changes

* Please log in to your Wagepoint account and click on "My Info" in the top menu of the page.
* Edit your home address in the appropriate fields.
* Click "Save" at the bottom of the page.
* Additionally, email [operations@hypha.coop](mailto:operations@hypha.coop) to inform the Operations Working Group of the change.

### Your banking information changes

* Please log in to your Wagepoint account and click on "Bank Info" in the top menu of the page.
* In the table containing your banking information, under the "Actions" column, click on the pen/edit icon.
* Edit your banking information in the appropriate fields.
* Click "Save Account" at the bottom of the pop-up screen.

### Your SIN changes

* Please email [operations@hypha.coop](mailto:operations@hypha.coop), and they can make the change from the admin side.

### Your base salary changes

* Nothing to do on your end — the Operations Working Group will take care of this change.

### You leave Hypha

* Nothing to do on your end — your personal email address is already in the Wagepoint portal, so your final paystub and your T4 can be sent directly to your personal email account when they're ready.
