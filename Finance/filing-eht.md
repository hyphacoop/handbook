# Filing EHT

Hypha files Employee Health Tax (EHT) amounts with the Government of Ontario once a year. The deadline for filing EHT is March 15th.

## Preparing EHT

The bookkeeper will prepare the EHT amount after the T4 is submitted to the CRA and the EHT Return Statement is received. The bookkeeper will send an e-mail to the Finance WG with the balance owing, amounts to fill out during filing, and the deadline to file and pay.

This will trigger Finance to take action.

## EHT Filing

To file EHT you will need to use an account registered with the Ontario Online Services website.

1. Visit the [ONT-TAXS online](https://www.ontario.ca/page/ont-taxs-online) website.
2. Click Sign-in to ONT-TAXS online
3. Click "File or View Returns and Instalments"
4. Click "File Now" in the current year for EHT Return
5. Total Ontario Remuneration: Enter line 14 of the T4 Summary (Employment Income)
6. Exemption Calculation: Request $1,000,000
7. The website will calculate the taxable amount, tax rate, total tax payable, and the balance due
8. Submit the report
9. Save a PDF of the EHT Return to [Google Drive](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1rvVpZM-kOTNIo6bocepFpqxCgH8jJsxZ?usp=drive_link)

 
## EHT Payment

If payment is required for EHT, you will need to coordinate with another member that has signing authority at the bank. The payment must be confirmed by the 2nd person within 48 hours.

1. Login to Desjardins
2. Select the `Payments` button on the right, a pulldown menu will appear
3. Select `Government remittances - Can-Act`
4. In the `Registered payments and accounts` page, clck `Pay` next to `Ontario Employer Health Tax -- ONEHT`
4. Reporting for month of: enter the last month of the year being reported
5. Taxable Ontario remuneration: enter the amount from the EHT Return.
6. Tax due: enter the amount from the EHT Return.
7. Payment amount: enter the tax dure amount
8. Select `Next`
9. Confirm values are correct
10. Select `Submit`
11. Confirm message `The transaction is now in Pending Approval status and requires 1 approver(s).` Displays at the top
12. Click `Done`
